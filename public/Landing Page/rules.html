Eating Ball - Game Rules

How to Play

  1. Control your ball using the arrow keys, mouse pointer, or your finger.
  2. Eat smaller balls to grow bigger.
  3. Avoid larger balls to survive.
  4. The game ends if your ball is eaten.

Power-Ups

  • Speed Boost — Temporarily increases the speed of blue balls.
  • Double Blue Balls — Temporarily doubles the number of blue balls.
    (Green ball only appears in AI Mode)
  • Shrink Red Balls — Temporarily shrinks all red balls, making them easier to eat.
  • Freeze Red Balls — Temporarily freezes all red balls in place.
  • Slow Red Balls — Temporarily slows down all red balls.
  • Instant Ball Eat — Eats all of the red balls on the screen instantly.
Some power-ups (like Double Blue Balls) are only available in AI Mode!
